👀 Gaze-Powered Canvas

The primary brush is defined by your vision. AirEye© tracks your eye coordinates in real-time, mapping your gaze directly to fluid strokes on the visual canvas.

Protocol: Precision Eye Tracking

👄 Mouth Trigger: Control

Perform a sequential action to manage your brush: OPEN then CLOSE your mouth to either BEGIN drawing, or to STOP. The system confirms the command at each state.

Trigger 1: Open-Close Mouth (Draw/Stop)

☝️ Kinetic Eraser

Activate the dynamic deletion tool with precise kinetic input. Extend only your index finger over any point on the canvas to execute a localized erase.

Gesture: Index Finger Only

🤏 Line Manipulation

Perform a precise pinch gesture directly over a drawn line segment to select and activate it. Keep your fingers pinched to drag and reposition the element.

Gesture: Pinch to Grab/Move

🎙️ Voice Protocol

Execute global commands with voice logic over a prioritized audio channel. Speak loudly and clearly to override current system aesthetic states and canvas data.
